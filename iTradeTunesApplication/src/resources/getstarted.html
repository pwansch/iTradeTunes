Terminology: The source computer or iPod is where the songs are now. The target computer is where you want the songs to be.

To use iSyncTunes:

  1. Source computer or iPod: Click Share or connect your iPod.
  2. Target computer: Add a remote library or iPod pointing to the source computer or iPod root.
  3. Target computer: Select songs.
  4. Target computer: Click Import.
  5. Click Add to iTunes or enable Add imported songs directly to iTunes and Delete songs from import library after adding them to iTunes in the user preferences to automate this step.

To view the complete Help, click Launch Help from the help menu.
